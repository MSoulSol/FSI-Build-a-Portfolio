About Me

An intellectual curiosity since childhood has fed into both a love for academics and novels. I regularly contribute articles of book review and literary criticism to my Goodreads profile, or my personal blog, The Salad Bowl by Cruton. Expanding upon an inclination for writing, I have also regularly published news, commentary, and events essays via various outlets, ranging from magazines to other blogs and social media sites. Finally, in further demonstrating my intellectual prowess and interest, I do have one research paper published in an academic peer-reviewed periodical. Revealing aptitude, that paper was one of two submitted as bachelor's-of-art theses.

This energy has also found its place in the world of art. I have contributed works to DeviantArt, in addition to designing mobile phone covers.
